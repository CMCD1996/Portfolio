### **Training**

I followed the subsequent training schedule over the 2018/2019 summer when training for my first Ironman.
Approximate training time per week was 20 hours for 12 weeks (240 hours).
Training preceding this schedule was skills based, approximately 8 hours per week for 45 weeks (360 hours). 
Total training time was approximately 600 hours.

Time | Mon | Tue | Wed | Thur | Fri | Sat | Sun                                                                           
:---:|:----:|:----:|:----:|:----:|:-----:|:-----:|:----------:
AM   | Swim | Run  | Swim | Run  | Swim  | Swim  | Bike + Run
PM   | Run  | Bike | Run  | Bike | Break | Break | Break

### **Tauranga Half 2019**

I competed in the Tauranga Half Multi Sport event in January 2019. This was my first triathlon race. The race includes a 1.9k swim, 90km cycle and 21km run. The swim was my first open water swim. I had issues with sighting but persevered. The cycle was three circuits from Mt Maunganui down the straight towards Papamoa and back. The run included a couple laps around the base of Mt Maunganui in approximately 30 degrees celcius.
